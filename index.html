library

The School provides excellent library and reading room facilities. It is well-stocked with books, covering all spheres of activities and varied tastes. The School subscribes to many magazines and newspapers.
Reading Room facilities are provided within the Library and students are encouraged to utilize this facility. The students have two compulsory periods for library per week. Each student is allowed to borrow two to three books at a time. Library is computerized. It has a collection of video, audio-tapes and CDs

what's new

  • Computer labs

    The school has two dedicated computer labs one senior students and other for junior classstudents with latest computers and experianced and dedicated computer faculties. In computer labs we provide latest software support for practice and developing IT skills both labs have facility of internet which help the students to know new discoveries in various subjects. The whole school building is connected through LAN and has dedicated server rooms.

  • Herbal Garden

    The school has Herbal Garden, which has medicinal plants. This garden enables the students to learn medicinal value of plants. Herbal garden is of immense value to educate the children about the ancient treasure of Indian Ayurvedic way of treatment. It gives the student a better understanding of medicinal value of plants. Students are taken by the Science Teachers to the herbal garden, where the scientific names and the uses of these medicinal plants are also displayed.The school has promoted the noble concept of “Harit Rajasthan”. Under this project 1000 saplings are being planted under NREGA. The project has provided adequate greenery to school campus and has provided a pollution free environment to the children.
